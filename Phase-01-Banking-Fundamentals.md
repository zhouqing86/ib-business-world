## 1.1 银行类型 / Types of Banks

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Commercial Bank** | 商业银行 | 接受存款并发放贷款的传统银行，例如工商银行、汇丰银行。Accept deposits and make loans; e.g., ICBC, HSBC. |
| **Investment Bank** | 投资银行 | 提供证券承销、并购顾问、自营交易等服务；例如高盛、摩根士丹利。Underwrite securities, advise on M&A, conduct proprietary trading; e.g., Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley. |
| **Central Bank** | 中央银行 | 负责货币政策、发行货币、充当最后贷款人；例如美联储、中国人民银行、香港金管局。Sets monetary policy, issues currency, acts as lender of last resort; e.g., Fed, PBOC, HKMA. |
| **Retail Bank** | 零售银行 | 面向个人消费者提供储蓄、贷款、支付等基础金融服务。Serves individual consumers with savings, loans, and payments. |
| **Private Bank** | 私人银行 | 为高净值个人（HNWI）提供定制化财富管理服务。Provides bespoke wealth management for high-net-worth individuals (HNWIs). |
| **Custodian Bank** | 托管银行 | 代表机构客户安全保管证券资产，并办理结算、公司行动处理。Holds and administers securities on behalf of institutional clients; handles settlement and corporate actions. |
| **Correspondent Bank** | 代理行 | 在跨境支付中代表另一家银行执行金融交易。Acts on behalf of another bank in cross-border financial transactions. |
| **Universal Bank** | 全能银行 | 同时提供零售、商业和投资银行服务的综合性银行；例如德意志银行、巴克莱银行。Combines retail, commercial, and investment banking under one roof; e.g., Deutsche Bank, Barclays. |
| **Shadow Bank** | 影子银行 | 从事与商业银行类似活动但不受同等监管的非银行金融机构，例如货币市场基金、对冲基金。Non-bank financial institutions performing bank-like functions (lending, maturity transformation) but outside traditional banking regulation; e.g., money market funds, hedge funds. |

## 1.2 核心资产负债表术语 / Core Balance Sheet Terms

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Assets** | 资产 | 银行拥有或控制的一切具有经济价值的项目，包括贷款、证券等。Everything of economic value owned or controlled by the bank, including loans and securities. |
| **Liabilities** | 负债 | 银行对外的债务义务，包括客户存款、发行债券等。Obligations owed to others, including customer deposits and issued bonds. |
| **Equity / Shareholders' Equity** | 股东权益 | 资产减负债后的净额，代表股东的剩余权益。Assets minus liabilities; represents residual ownership interest of shareholders. |
| **Tier 1 Capital** | 一级资本 | 最高质量的监管资本，主要包括普通股和留存收益。Highest-quality regulatory capital; primarily common equity and retained earnings. |
| **CET1 — Common Equity Tier 1** | 核心一级资本 | 一级资本中质量最高的部分，包括普通股本和账面留存收益。The highest-quality component of Tier 1 capital: common shares and retained earnings. |
| **Tier 2 Capital** | 二级资本 | 次级债务等补充资本工具。Supplementary capital instruments such as subordinated debt. |
| **RWA — Risk-Weighted Assets** | 风险加权资产 | 将各类资产按其风险权重调整后的总资产，用于衡量资本充足率。Total assets weighted by their risk level; used to measure capital adequacy. |
| **Leverage Ratio** | 杠杆率 | 一级资本除以未加权的总风险敞口，衡量银行的杠杆程度。Tier 1 capital divided by total unweighted exposure; a non-risk-based capital measure. |
| **Book Value** | 账面价值 | 资产按历史成本在账簿上记录的价值。Value of an asset as recorded on the books at historical cost. |
| **Market Value** | 市场价值 | 资产在流动市场中可交易的当前价格。Current price at which an asset can be traded in the open market. |
| **Mark-to-Market (MTM)** | 按市值计价 | 将资产或负债按当前市场价格重新估值的会计方法。Revaluing assets or liabilities at current market prices; also called "fair value accounting". |
| **Mark-to-Model** | 按模型计价 | 对于缺乏市场报价的资产，使用内部模型估算公允价值。Using internal models to estimate fair value for illiquid assets that lack observable market prices. |

## 1.3 常用财务指标缩略词 / Common Financial Metric Abbreviations

| 缩写 | 全称 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|------|--------------------|
| **P&L** | Profit & Loss | 损益表 | 记录一段时期内收入与支出的财务报表。Financial statement showing revenues and expenses over a period. |
| **AUM** | Assets Under Management | 资产管理规模 | 金融机构代客户管理的总资产价值。Total value of assets managed on behalf of clients. |
| **NAV** | Net Asset Value | 净资产值 | 基金总资产减总负债后的价值，通常以每份额计算。Fund's total assets minus liabilities; often expressed per unit/share. |
| **ROE** | Return on Equity | 股本回报率 | 净利润除以股东权益，衡量股东资本回报效率。Net income divided by shareholders' equity; measures how efficiently equity is used to generate profit. |
| **ROA** | Return on Assets | 资产回报率 | 净利润除以总资产，衡量资产盈利能力。Net income divided by total assets; measures how efficiently assets generate profit. |
| **RORWA** | Return on Risk-Weighted Assets | 风险加权资产回报率 | 净利润除以风险加权资产，银行业常用盈利效率指标。Net income divided by RWA; a profitability metric commonly used in banking. |
| **NIM** | Net Interest Margin | 净息差 | 净利息收入除以生息资产，反映银行利息业务盈利能力。Net interest income as a percentage of interest-earning assets. |
| **NII** | Net Interest Income | 净利息收入 | 利息收入减利息支出后的净额。Interest earned minus interest paid. |
| **EBITDA** | Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation & Amortization | 息税折旧摊销前利润 | 衡量公司核心经营盈利能力的指标，常用于公司估值。A measure of core operating profitability, widely used in corporate valuation. |
| **EPS** | Earnings Per Share | 每股收益 | 净利润除以流通股数，衡量每股的盈利能力。Net income divided by outstanding shares; measures per-share profitability. |
| **PE** | Price-to-Earnings Ratio | 市盈率 | 股价除以每股收益，反映市场对公司盈利的估值倍数。Stock price divided by EPS; reflects how much investors pay per dollar of earnings. |
| **PB** | Price-to-Book Ratio | 市净率 | 股价除以每股账面净资产，评估股票相对账面价值的溢价。Stock price divided by book value per share; assesses premium over book value. |
| **BPS** | Basis Points | 基点 | 利率/收益率的最小度量单位，1 BPS = 0.01%，100 BPS = 1%。Smallest unit for interest rates/yields: 1 bps = 0.01%; 100 bps = 1%. |
| **PV** | Present Value | 现值 | 未来现金流按折现率折算回今天的价值。The current worth of a future cash flow, discounted at a given rate. |
| **FV** | Future Value | 未来值 | 今天的资金在未来某一时刻的价值（含利息增长）。The value of a current amount at a future date, accounting for interest growth. |
| **NPV** | Net Present Value | 净现值 | 所有未来现金流的现值之和减去初始投资。Sum of all discounted future cash flows minus the initial investment; used in capital budgeting. |
| **IRR** | Internal Rate of Return | 内部收益率 | 使投资净现值等于零的折现率，越高越好。Discount rate that makes NPV equal to zero; higher IRR indicates better return. |
| **DV01** | Dollar Value of 1 Basis Point | 基点美元价值 | 利率变动1个基点（0.01%）时，债券价格的变动金额。The change in a bond's price for a 1 basis point change in yield; also called PVBP. |
| **PVBP** | Price Value of a Basis Point | 基点价格值 | 同DV01，利率变化1个基点导致的价格变动。Same as DV01; the price change caused by a 1 bps yield move. |

## 1.4 基础交易与账户术语 / Basic Trading & Account Terms

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Long** | 做多 / 持有多仓 | 持有资产，预期价格上涨获利。Holding an asset with the expectation that its price will rise. |
| **Short** | 做空 | 先借入并卖出资产，预期价格下跌后低价买回获利。Borrowing and selling an asset, expecting to buy it back at a lower price to profit. |
| **Cover** | 平空 | 买回已做空的证券，平仓空头头寸。Buying back a previously sold short position to close it. |
| **Unwind** | 平仓 / 解仓 | 关闭已有头寸，平掉持有的多头或空头。Closing out an existing position (long or short). |
| **Position** | 头寸 / 仓位 | 持有某一资产的数量或方向（多/空）。The quantity and direction (long/short) of an asset held. |
| **Exposure** | 敞口 / 风险暴露 | 对某一风险因素的潜在损失量。The amount at risk to a particular risk factor. |
| **Notional** | 名义本金 | 衍生品合约中用于计算支付金额的参考本金，通常不实际交换。Reference principal amount used to calculate payments in derivatives; often not physically exchanged. |
| **Principal** | 本金 | 借贷或投资的原始金额（不含利息）。The original amount of a loan or investment, excluding interest. |
| **Counterparty** | 交易对手 | 金融交易中的另一方。The other party in a financial transaction. |
| **Broker** | 经纪商 | 代理客户执行买卖交易、收取佣金的中介机构。Intermediary who executes trades on behalf of clients in exchange for commission. |
| **Dealer** | 交易商 | 以自身账户买卖证券的金融机构，通常同时充当做市商。A firm that trades securities for its own account; often acts as a market maker. |
| **Custodian** | 托管人 | 代替投资者安全保管证券和现金的机构。Institution safeguarding securities and cash on behalf of investors. |
| **Nostro Account** | 我方在他方开立的账户 | 银行A在银行B开立的本币或外币账户，用于对账及结算。Bank A's account held at Bank B, used for reconciliation and settlement. "Nostro" means "ours" in Latin. |
| **Vostro Account** | 他方在我处开立的账户 | Nostro的镜像：银行B在自身系统内为银行A持有的账户。Mirror of nostro: Bank B's record of the account it holds for Bank A. "Vostro" means "yours" in Latin. |
| **Omnibus Account** | 综合账户 | 将多个客户的资产合并存放在一个账户下，由托管人统一管理。A single account holding assets of multiple clients, managed collectively by a custodian. |
