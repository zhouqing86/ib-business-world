# Phase 11 — Global Regulatory Framework

> **学习目标 / Learning Objectives**  
> 了解国际和主要地区监管机构的职能与重要法规，掌握反洗钱（AML）合规要求。  
> Understand the roles of key global and regional regulators, major regulatory frameworks, and AML/KYC compliance requirements.

## 11.1 国际监管机构 / International Regulatory Bodies

| 缩写 | 全称 | 中文 | 职能 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **BIS** | Bank for International Settlements | 国际清算银行 | 被称为"央行的央行"，为各国央行的定期会晤和合作提供平台；旗下巴塞尔银行监管委员会（BCBS）制定国际银行监管标准（巴塞尔协议）。"Bank of central banks"; provides forum for central bank cooperation; BCBS develops international banking standards (Basel Accords). |
| **FSB** | Financial Stability Board | 金融稳定理事会 | G20成员国金融监管机构组成的国际机构，负责监测和应对全球金融体系的系统性风险；指定全球系统重要性银行（G-SIBs）名单。International body coordinating national financial authorities; monitors global systemic risks; designates G-SIBs. |
| **IOSCO** | International Organization of Securities Commissions | 国际证监会组织 | 全球证券监管机构的国际合作组织，制定证券市场监管原则和标准。International body bringing together securities regulators; develops regulatory principles for capital markets. |
| **FATF** | Financial Action Task Force | 金融行动特别工作组 | 制定全球反洗钱（AML）和反恐融资（CFT）标准的政府间机构；发布白名单/灰名单/黑名单。Intergovernmental body setting international AML/CFT standards; publishes compliance lists (white/grey/blacklists). |

## 11.2 巴塞尔协议框架 / Basel Accord Framework

| 术语 | 中文 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| **Basel I (1988)** | 巴塞尔协议I | 第一个国际银行资本充足协议，引入风险加权资产（RWA）概念，要求8%最低资本充足率。First international capital adequacy framework; introduced RWA; required 8% minimum total capital ratio. |
| **Basel II (2004)** | 巴塞尔协议II | 引入三大支柱：Pillar 1（最低资本要求）、Pillar 2（监管审查程序）、Pillar 3（市场信息披露）。Three-pillar structure: Pillar 1 (minimum capital), Pillar 2 (supervisory review), Pillar 3 (market disclosure). |
| **Basel III (2010-2019)** | 巴塞尔协议III | 金融危机后改革：提高CET1要求、引入杠杆率、LCR、NSFR；资本质量和数量同步提升。Post-GFC reform: raised CET1 requirements, introduced leverage ratio, LCR, NSFR; improved capital quality and quantity. |
| **Basel IV (2017+)** | 巴塞尔协议IV (最终版) | 进一步改革内部模型法（限制与标准法的偏差），引入输出底限（Output Floor：内部模型结果不低于标准法的72.5%）。Further reform restricting use of internal models (IRB); output floor: IRB RWA ≥ 72.5% of standardised approach RWA. |
| **Pillar 1 — Minimum Capital** | 第一支柱 — 最低资本 | 规定信用风险、市场风险和操作风险的最低资本计算标准方法。Sets minimum capital for credit, market, and operational risks using standardised and internal model approaches. |
| **Pillar 2 — SREP** | 第二支柱 — 监管审查评估程序 | 监管机构对银行个体风险状况（含Pillar 1未覆盖风险）进行审查，可要求额外资本（Pillar 2 Requirement, P2R）。Supervisory review of bank-specific risks; may impose additional capital (P2R) beyond Pillar 1 minimums. |
| **Pillar 3 — Disclosure** | 第三支柱 — 信息披露 | 要求银行向市场公开披露资本结构、风险状况等信息，加强市场纪律约束。Requires banks to publicly disclose capital structure and risk profile to enhance market discipline. |

## 11.3 美国监管框架 / US Regulatory Framework

| 机构/法规 | 中文 | 说明 |
|-----------|------|------|
| **Federal Reserve (Fed)** | 美联储 | 美国中央银行；双重职责：维持最大就业和物价稳定；监管美国银行控股公司和国际银行在美分支机构。US central bank; dual mandate (maximum employment + price stability); supervises bank holding companies and foreign bank branches. |
| **OCC** | 货币监理署 | 监管所有在美国运营的联邦特许银行（国家银行）；在财政部下设。Regulates all federally chartered (national) banks operating in the US; under Treasury Dept. |
| **FDIC** | 联邦存款保险公司 | 为银行存款提供保险（最高$250,000每账户）；同时担任问题银行的清算机构。Insures bank deposits (up to $250,000 per account per bank); also acts as receiver for failed banks. |
| **SEC** | 证券交易委员会 | 监管美国证券市场、上市公司信息披露、投资顾问和投资公司。Regulates US securities markets, public company disclosure, investment advisers, and investment companies. |
| **FINRA** | 金融业监管局 | 监管美国证券经纪商和交易商的行业自律组织（SRO），负责经纪商注册和合规监察。SRO regulating US broker-dealers; handles registration, examination, and enforcement. |
| **CFTC** | 商品期货交易委员会 | 监管美国期货、期权及场外衍生品（互换）市场。Regulates US futures, options, and OTC derivatives (swaps) markets. |
| **Dodd-Frank Act (2010)** | 多德-弗兰克法案 | 2008年金融危机后美国最重要的金融改革立法；创设金融稳定监督委员会（FSOC）、消费者金融保护局（CFPB）；强制集中清算场外衍生品；实施沃克尔规则。Post-GFC landmark US financial reform: created FSOC, CFPB; mandated central clearing of OTC derivatives; implemented Volcker Rule. |
| **Volcker Rule** | 沃克尔规则 | 禁止银行利用自有资金进行自营交易（Proprietary Trading）并限制对对冲基金和PE的投资；保罗·沃克尔（前美联储主席）提议。Prohibits banks from proprietary trading and restricts investments in hedge funds/PE funds using bank capital; proposed by former Fed Chair Paul Volcker. |
| **FATCA** | 海外账户税收合规法案 | 要求外国金融机构向美国国税局（IRS）报告美国人持有的境外账户信息；不合规则对来自美国的付款预扣30%税款。Requires foreign financial institutions to report US account holders' information to the IRS; non-compliance triggers 30% withholding tax on US-sourced payments. |

## 11.4 欧洲监管框架 / European Regulatory Framework

| 法规/机构 | 中文 | 说明 |
|-----------|------|------|
| **ECB (European Central Bank)** | 欧洲中央银行 | 欧元区货币政策机构；同时通过SSM（单一监管机制）直接监管欧元区大型银行。Eurozone central bank; also directly supervises major Eurozone banks via SSM (Single Supervisory Mechanism). |
| **MiFID II** | 金融工具市场指令II | 欧盟最重要的证券市场法规（2018年实施）：提高交易透明度、强化最佳执行要求、将交易量限制在暗池、研究费用与执行费用拆分（Unbundling）。Major EU securities regulation (2018): improves transparency, mandates best execution, limits dark pool trading, requires research/execution cost unbundling. |
| **EMIR** | 欧洲市场基础设施监管 | 要求OTC衍生品集中清算（通过CCP）、向交易存储库报告、采用风险缓释技术（保证金）；对应美国Dodd-Frank的衍生品改革。Mandates central clearing of OTC derivatives via CCPs, trade reporting to trade repositories, and risk mitigation (margining); EU equivalent of Dodd-Frank's derivative reform. |
| **CSDR** | 中央证券存管条例 | 规范欧盟中央证券存管机构（CSD）运营标准；引入结算纪律措施（强制买入和现金罚款）。Regulates EU CSDs; introduces settlement discipline measures (mandatory buy-ins and cash penalties for fails). |
| **GDPR** | 通用数据保护条例 | 欧盟个人数据保护法规；对金融机构收集、处理和存储客户个人数据的方式设有严格要求；违规最高罚款全球营业额4%。EU data protection regulation; strict rules on collecting, processing, and storing personal data; max fine: 4% of global annual turnover. |
| **CRD IV / CRR** | 资本要求指令IV / 资本要求条例 | 将巴塞尔III在欧盟的法律实施框架：CRD（指令，需各国立法转化）+CRR（条例，直接适用）。EU implementation of Basel III: CRD (Directive, requires national transposition) + CRR (Regulation, directly applicable). |
| **SFDR** | 可持续金融信息披露条例 | 要求EU金融机构披露投资产品的ESG（环境、社会和公司治理）属性；引入Article 6/8/9产品分类。Requires EU financial institutions to disclose ESG characteristics of investment products; introduces Article 6/8/9 product classification. |

## 11.5 反洗钱与合规 / AML & Compliance

| 术语 | 中文 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| **AML (Anti-Money Laundering)** | 反洗钱 | 识别和防止将非法所得转化为合法资金的系列程序和控制措施。Procedures and controls to detect and prevent conversion of illegally obtained funds into legitimate money. |
| **CFT (Combating Financing of Terrorism)** | 反恐融资 | 识别和阻止向恐怖主义活动提供资金的措施。Measures to detect and prevent funding of terrorist activities. |
| **KYC (Know Your Customer)** | 了解你的客户 | 金融机构在建立业务关系前，核实客户身份和了解其业务性质的义务要求。Obligation for financial institutions to verify client identity and understand the nature of their business before establishing relationships. |
| **CDD (Customer Due Diligence)** | 客户尽职调查 | KYC的核心组成部分：核查客户身份、了解业务目的和预期交易特征、持续监控。Core component of KYC: verifying identity, understanding business purpose, and ongoing monitoring. |
| **EDD (Enhanced Due Diligence)** | 加强尽职调查 | 对高风险客户（PEP、高风险地区客户）进行更深入的尽职调查和持续监控。More in-depth due diligence for high-risk clients (PEPs, clients from high-risk jurisdictions). |
| **PEP (Politically Exposed Person)** | 政治公众人物 | 担任（或曾担任）重要公职的人员（政府官员、军队高层等）及其家庭成员；金融机构须对PEP进行EDD。Individuals holding (or having held) prominent public positions (government officials, senior military, etc.) and their family members; require EDD. |
| **SAR (Suspicious Activity Report)** | 可疑活动报告 | 金融机构向监管机构（如香港海关/金融情报组JFIU）提交的针对可疑交易或客户行为的报告。Report filed by financial institutions to regulators (e.g., HK JFIU) when suspicious transactions or activities are identified. |
| **OFAC (Office of Foreign Assets Control)** | 美国外国资产控制办公室 | 美国财政部下属机构，负责管理和执行美国经济和贸易制裁计划（针对国家、实体和个人）。US Treasury unit administering and enforcing US economic and trade sanctions (against countries, entities, individuals). |
| **Sanctions Screening** | 制裁名单筛查 | 金融机构须对所有客户和交易对手进行制裁名单（OFAC SDN、UN Security Council、EU）实时筛查。Checking all clients and counterparties against sanctions lists (OFAC SDN, UN, EU) in real time. |
| **STR (Suspicious Transaction Report)** | 可疑交易报告 | 香港等地区对可疑洗钱/恐怖融资交易的强制报告机制。Mandatory report filed in HK and other jurisdictions when suspicious ML/TF transactions are identified. |
