# Phase 12 — Investment Banking & Capital Markets

> **学习目标 / Learning Objectives**  
> 理解投资银行的核心业务线——股权融资（ECM）、债券融资（DCM）、并购（M&A）和杠杆融资（Leveraged Finance）的流程与术语。  
> Understand core investment banking business lines: ECM, DCM, M&A, and leveraged finance processes and terminology.

## 12.1 投资银行业务概览 / IB Business Overview

投资银行的核心业务分为三类：  
The core IB business lines can be categorised as:

```
Investment Banking Division (IBD)
├── Capital Markets
│   ├── ECM — Equity Capital Markets (股权资本市场)
│   └── DCM — Debt Capital Markets (债务资本市场)
├── M&A Advisory (并购顾问)
└── Leveraged Finance (杠杆融资)
```

## 12.2 股权资本市场 (ECM) / Equity Capital Markets

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **ECM** | 股权资本市场部 | 协助公司通过发行股票融资的投行业务部门，包括IPO、二次发行、可转债等。Business line helping companies raise equity capital via IPO, follow-ons, CBs, etc. |
| **IPO (Initial Public Offering)** | 首次公开发行 | 公司首次向公众出售股份并在证券交易所挂牌的过程；通常包括路演（Roadshow）、簿记建档（Bookbuild）和定价（Pricing）。Company's first sale of shares to the public and listing on an exchange; process includes roadshow, bookbuild, and pricing. |
| **Bookbuilding** | 簿记建档 | 承销商向机构投资者询价，收集投资意向（认购订单）以确定发行价格的过程。Process where underwriters gauge investor demand by taking orders from institutional investors to determine the offering price. |
| **Bookrunner / Lead Manager** | 主承销商 | 负责管理整个发行过程（簿记、定价、分配）的主要承销银行。Lead bank managing the entire issuance process (bookbuilding, pricing, allocation). |
| **Co-Manager** | 联合管理行 | 协助主承销商销售股份但不负责簿记的承销团成员银行。Syndicate member banks that assist in distribution but do not run the books. |
| **Greenshoe Option / Over-Allotment Option** | 绿鞋期权 / 超额配股权 | 承销商在IPO后30天内可额外认购最多15%股份的期权，用于稳定股价；在主承销商做空超额认购部分时使用。Option allowing underwriters to sell up to 15% additional shares; used to stabilise the share price post-IPO. |
| **Cornerstone Investor** | 基石投资者 | 香港IPO特有：在公开招股前已承诺认购特定金额股份、并锁定持股6个月的策略性大投资者；提升IPO公信力。HK-specific: strategic investor(s) who commit to subscribe a fixed amount pre-IPO with a 6-month lock-up; enhances deal credibility. |
| **Anchor Investor** | 基础投资者 | 在路演前已认购特定比例股份的机构投资者，但锁定期通常较短（30天）或无锁定。Institutional investor committing to a specified allocation before roadshow; shorter or no lock-up vs. cornerstone. |
| **Lock-up Period** | 禁售期 | 股票上市后，内部人和早期投资者在此期间不得出售股票（通常180天）。Period after IPO during which insiders and early investors are restricted from selling shares (typically 180 days). |
| **Stabilization** | 价格稳定机制 | 承销商在IPO后通过买入股票（使用绿鞋期权）维持市场价格稳定的操作。Underwriter's activity buying shares in the aftermarket (using greenshoe) to support the stock price post-listing. |
| **Prospectus** | 招股说明书 | 向潜在投资者披露公司财务状况、业务模式、风险因素和发行条款的法律文件。Legal disclosure document for potential investors; covers financials, business model, risk factors, and offering terms. |

## 12.3 债务资本市场 (DCM) / Debt Capital Markets

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **DCM** | 债务资本市场部 | 协助政府和企业通过发行债券（公开市场）或安排银团贷款融资的投行业务部门。Business line helping governments and corporates raise debt capital via bond issuance or syndicated loans. |
| **Arranger** | 安排行 | 组织和协调债券发行或银团贷款的主要银行。Lead bank organising a bond issuance or loan syndication. |
| **Underwriter** | 承销商 | 承诺以约定价格全额认购发行证券（若市场认购不足，承销商自行持有）的银行/机构。Bank or institution committing to purchase the full issue at the agreed price if not fully subscribed by investors. |
| **Syndicated Loan** | 银团贷款 | 由多家银行联合提供的大额贷款，通常由牵头行（Mandated Lead Arranger/MLA）组织；适用于大型企业融资、并购融资等。Large loan provided by a group of banks; led by a Mandated Lead Arranger (MLA); used for large corporate financing, leveraged buyouts, etc. |
| **Road Show** | 路演 | 发行人高管团队与承销商一起拜访机构投资者的营销推介过程，用于建立投资者需求。Marketing process where issuer management and underwriters meet institutional investors to gauge and build demand. |
| **Pricing** | 定价 | 在充分掌握市场需求后，确定债券票息（或股票发行价）的最终决定步骤。Final step of determining the coupon/price after fully assessing investor demand. |
| **Allocation** | 份额分配 | 主承销商根据投资者认购意向、投资者质量和关系决定如何分配债券份额。Bookrunner's process of deciding how to distribute bond amounts among investors based on their orders, quality, and relationships. |
| **NIP (New Issue Premium)** | 新发溢价 | 新发行债券相对于同发行人二级市场现有债券所给予的额外收益率补偿，以吸引投资者认购。额外利差，通常5-20bps。Extra yield offered on a new bond vs. the issuer's existing secondary market bonds to incentivise demand; typically 5–20 bps. |
| **Cover Ratio** | 认购覆盖倍数 | 投资者认购总额除以发行规模；例如5倍认购表明需求旺盛。Total orders divided by deal size; e.g., 5x covered means very strong demand. |
| **Investment Grade (IG) Bond** | 投资级债券 | 评级BBB−/Baa3及以上的企业或政府债券；流动性好，买家广泛（含保险公司、养老基金等受限投资者）。Bonds rated BBB−/Baa3 or above; high liquidity; accessible to restricted investors (insurers, pension funds). |
| **High Yield (HY) Bond** | 高收益债券 | 评级低于BBB−/Baa3的"垃圾债"；收益率高但违约风险大；买家以对冲基金和高收益专项基金为主。Below investment grade ("junk") bonds; higher yield compensates for higher default risk; buyers include HY-dedicated funds and hedge funds. |

## 12.4 杠杆融资 / Leveraged Finance

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **LBO (Leveraged Buyout)** | 杠杆收购 | PE基金大量借债收购目标公司，以目标公司自身资产/现金流偿还债务的收购方式；债务通常占收购价的60-70%。PE acquisition using significant borrowed funds; the target's assets/cash flows service the debt; debt typically 60–70% of purchase price. |
| **Leveraged Loan** | 杠杆贷款 | 向信用级别低（通常低于B+）或已高度负债的公司提供的银行贷款；通常为浮动利率（SOFR + spread）；可转在二级市场交易。Loan to companies with below-investment-grade credit or high leverage; typically floating rate (SOFR + spread); tradeable in secondary market. |
| **TLB (Term Loan B)** | B类定期贷款 | LBO融资中最常见的杠杆贷款类型：期限7年，摊还比例低（每年1%），其余在到期时一次性偿还（Bullet）；主要由机构投资者（CLO、对冲基金）持有。Most common leveraged loan type in LBOs: 7-year tenor; minimal amortisation (1% p.a.); bullet maturity; held primarily by institutional investors (CLOs, hedge funds). |
| **Revolving Credit Facility (RCF)** | 循环信贷额度 | 企业可按需提取和偿还的备用信贷额度，用于营运资金和短期流动性需求。Committed credit line that can be drawn and repaid repeatedly; used for working capital and liquidity needs. |
| **CLO (Collateralized Loan Obligation)** | 担保贷款凭证 | 以杠杆贷款为资产池构建的证券化产品，分层（Tranche）发行；是杠杆贷款市场最大的买家群体。Securitisation vehicle backed by a pool of leveraged loans; largest single buyer of leveraged loans globally. |
| **Mezzanine Finance** | 夹层融资 | 介于优先债和股权之间的混合融资工具；风险/收益高于优先债但低于股权；可含PIK（实物偿付）成分。Hybrid financing between senior debt and equity; higher risk/return than senior debt but below equity; may include PIK component. |
| **PIK (Payment in Kind)** | 实物偿付 / 付息方式 | 以额外本金而非现金支付利息的安排；保留现金流但增加总负债。Interest paid by issuing additional notes/shares rather than cash; preserves cash flow but increases total debt. |
| **EBITDA Multiple** | EBITDA倍数 | 收购价格 / EBITDA；LBO定价和杠杆率（债务/EBITDA）的核心衡量指标。Purchase Price / EBITDA; key metric for deal pricing and leverage ratio (Total Debt / EBITDA) in LBOs. |
| **Covenant** | 契约条款 | 贷款协议中对借款人的约束条款，分为Maintenance Covenant（定期测试财务指标）和Incurrence Covenant（触发特定行为时测试）。Restrictions/undertakings in a loan agreement; Maintenance covenants tested periodically (e.g., quarterly); Incurrence covenants tested only on triggering events. |
| **Covenant-Lite (Cov-Lite)** | 轻契约贷款 | 只含Incurrence Covenant（无Maintenance Covenant）的杠杆贷款，对借款人更有利；在市场宽松时普遍。Leveraged loans with only incurrence covenants (no maintenance testing); borrower-friendly; became common in loose credit markets. |

## 12.5 并购 (M&A) / Mergers & Acquisitions

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Buy-Side M&A Advisory** | 买方并购顾问 | 代表收购方（买家）提供收购目标选择、估值、尽职调查和谈判支持的顾问服务。Advisory services for the acquirer: target screening, valuation, due diligence, negotiation support. |
| **Sell-Side M&A Advisory** | 卖方并购顾问 | 代表出售方（卖家）组织拍卖流程、寻找最优买家并最大化出售价值的顾问服务。Advisory services for the seller: running auction process, finding optimal buyers, maximising sale value. |
| **Due Diligence** | 尽职调查 | 收购方在完成交易前对目标公司的财务、法律、商业、税务等方面进行的全面调查。Comprehensive investigation by buyer into target company's financial, legal, commercial, and tax affairs before deal completion. |
| **Enterprise Value (EV)** | 企业价值 | EV = 市值 + 净债务（债务 − 现金）；反映收购整个企业（含债务）所需的总对价。EV = Market Cap + Net Debt (Debt − Cash); total cost of acquiring the whole enterprise including its debt. |
| **Equity Value** | 权益价值 | EV − 净债务 = 股东所持有的价值部分（即市值）。EV − Net Debt; the portion attributable to equity (i.e., market capitalisation). |
| **EV/EBITDA Multiple** | EV/EBITDA倍数 | 最常用的并购估值倍数；通过同类可比公司（Comps）和先例交易（Precedents）的倍数区间确定合理估值范围。Most common M&A valuation multiple; benchmarked against public comps and precedent transactions to derive a valuation range. |
| **DCF (Discounted Cash Flow)** | 现金流折现估值 | 以预测未来自由现金流、加上终值（Terminal Value），按加权平均资本成本（WACC）折现求和的估值方法。Valuation by forecasting future free cash flows + terminal value, discounted at WACC. |
| **WACC (Weighted Average Cost of Capital)** | 加权平均资本成本 | 债务和股权融资成本的加权平均值，用于DCF的折现率。Weighted average of the cost of equity and after-tax cost of debt; used as DCF discount rate. |
| **Fairness Opinion** | 公平意见书 | 独立投行就交易价格是否对股东公平发表的书面意见；通常用于防止董事会被指控违反受托责任。Written opinion from an independent bank on whether deal consideration is fair to shareholders; protects board against breach of fiduciary duty claims. |
| **Break Fee / Termination Fee** | 分手费 | 若交易当事方单方面终止协议，须向对方支付的预设赔偿金额（通常为交易价值的1-3%）。Pre-agreed compensation to be paid if a party withdraws from a deal unilaterally; typically 1–3% of deal value. |
| **White Knight** | 白衣骑士 | 在敌意收购中，被收购公司主动寻找的更受欢迎的替代收购方。In a hostile takeover, a more preferred acquirer invited by the target to rescue it from an unwanted bidder. |
| **Poison Pill** | 毒丸计划 | 目标公司的反收购防御措施：当收购方持股超过一定比例时，触发现有股东以折价购买新股的权利，稀释收购方股权。Anti-takeover defence: triggers rights for existing shareholders to buy shares at discount when a hostile bidder exceeds a threshold; dilutes the bidder. |
