# Phase 04 — Equity Markets

## 4.1 股票基础 / Equity Basics

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Common Stock / Ordinary Share** | 普通股 | 拥有公司所有权的基本股份，享有投票权和剩余分红权。Basic ownership units of a company; carry voting rights and rights to residual dividends. |
| **Preferred Stock / Preference Share** | 优先股 | 股息优先于普通股派发，通常无投票权，清算时优先受偿。Dividends paid before common stock; usually no voting rights; priority in liquidation. |
| **Authorized Shares** | 授权股份 | 公司章程允许发行的最大股份总数。Maximum number of shares a company is permitted to issue under its charter. |
| **Issued Shares** | 已发行股份 | 公司实际向股东发行的股份总数。Total shares actually issued to shareholders. |
| **Outstanding Shares** | 流通股 | 已发行但未被公司回购的股份数量，用于计算EPS和市值。Issued shares minus treasury stock; used to calculate EPS and market cap. |
| **Treasury Stock** | 库藏股 | 公司从市场买回并持有的自家股票，不参与分红和投票。Shares repurchased by the company; held in treasury; no voting rights or dividends. |
| **Market Capitalization** | 市值 | 流通股数乘以当前股价。Outstanding shares × current stock price. |
| **Free Float** | 自由流通股 / 自由流通市值 | 可供市场公开交易的股份比例，扣除大股东、内部人所持锁定股份。Proportion of shares available for public trading, excluding locked-up shares held by insiders/major shareholders. |
| **ADR (American Depositary Receipt)** | 美国存托凭证 | 代表外国公司股票的凭证，在美国交易所上市交易，方便美国投资者持有外国股票。Certificates representing shares of a foreign company, listed on a US exchange; enables US investors to hold foreign stocks. |
| **GDR (Global Depositary Receipt)** | 全球存托凭证 | 类似ADR但可在多个国际交易所交易。Similar to ADR but listed on multiple international exchanges. |
| **H-Share** | H股 | 在香港联交所上市的中国内地公司股份，以港元定价。Shares of mainland China companies listed on HKEX; priced in HKD. |
| **A-Share** | A股 | 在上海或深圳交易所上市、以人民币交易的股票，主要面向境内投资者（境外机构可通过陆股通或QFII参与）。Shares listed on SSE/SZSE; priced in RMB; primarily for domestic investors, but accessible to foreign institutions via Stock Connect or QFII. |
| **B-Share** | B股 | 在上海（以美元计价）或深圳（以港元计价）上市的境外投资者专用股份，目前流动性较低。Shares listed on SSE (USD) or SZSE (HKD) for foreign investors; now relatively illiquid. |

## 4.2 企业行动 / Corporate Actions

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **IPO (Initial Public Offering)** | 首次公开发行 | 公司首次向公众发售股份并在证券交易所挂牌上市。Company's first sale of shares to the public and listing on a stock exchange. |
| **Follow-on / Secondary Offering** | 二次/增发 | 已上市公司再次向市场增发股票（可能摊薄现有股东）。Additional shares issued by a listed company (may dilute existing shareholders). |
| **Rights Issue** | 供股 / 配股 | 向现有股东按持股比例以折价认购新股的方式筹集资金。Existing shareholders offered new shares at a discount, in proportion to their holdings. |
| **Stock Split** | 股票分拆 | 将每股分成多股，价格按比例降低，总市值不变（例如2:1拆股）。Dividing each share into multiple shares; price falls proportionally; market cap unchanged (e.g., 2-for-1 split). |
| **Reverse Split** | 股票合并 | 将多股合并为一股，价格上升，通常用于防止退市（例如1:10合并）。Combining multiple shares into one; price rises; used to avoid delisting (e.g., 1-for-10 reverse split). |
| **Cash Dividend** | 现金股息 | 公司将利润以现金形式分配给股东。Distribution of profits to shareholders in cash. |
| **Stock Dividend** | 股票股息 | 以额外股票形式分配给现有股东，而非现金。Distribution of additional shares instead of cash. |
| **Spinoff** | 分拆上市 | 母公司将子公司分离成独立上市公司，现有股东按比例获得子公司股份。Separating a subsidiary into an independent listed company; existing shareholders receive shares in the new entity. |
| **Merger & Acquisition (M&A)** | 并购 | 两家公司合并（Merger）或一家收购另一家（Acquisition）的企业行为。Combining companies (Merger) or one company buying another (Acquisition). |
| **Share Buyback / Repurchase** | 股票回购 | 公司从市场回购自家股票，可减少流通股数量，提升每股收益。Company purchasing its own shares in the market; reduces share count, potentially boosting EPS. |
| **Delisting** | 退市 | 股票从交易所摘牌，不再公开交易。Removal of a stock from a stock exchange; shares can no longer be publicly traded. |
| **Record Date** | 股权登记日 | 确定哪些股东有权获得股息或参与企业行动的截止日期。Date used to determine shareholders entitled to dividends or corporate action benefits. |
| **Ex-Dividend Date** | 除息日 | 在此日或之后购入股票的投资者无权获得即将派发的股息。Date on or after which a buyer of shares is not entitled to the upcoming dividend. |

## 4.3 权益衍生品 / Equity Derivatives

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Equity Call Option** | 股票认购期权 | 赋予持有人在到期日前以行权价买入特定股票的权利（非义务）。Right (not obligation) to buy a stock at the strike price on or before expiry. |
| **Equity Put Option** | 股票认沽期权 | 赋予持有人在到期日前以行权价卖出特定股票的权利（非义务）。Right (not obligation) to sell a stock at the strike price on or before expiry. |
| **Warrant** | 权证 | 由发行人（通常是公司或银行）发行的长期期权，行权时通常发行新股或现金结算。Long-term option issued by a company or bank; exercise may result in new share issuance or cash settlement. |
| **ELN (Equity-Linked Note)** | 股权挂钩票据 | 回报与特定股票或股票指数挂钩的结构性票据。Structured note whose return is linked to the performance of one or more equities or an equity index. |
| **CBBC (Callable Bull/Bear Contract)** | 牛熊证 | 香港特有的杠杆产品，追踪股票或指数，含强制回收机制（触发价）。HK-specific leveraged instrument tracking a stock or index; has a mandatory call feature (knock-out level). |
| **Turbo Warrant** | 涡轮 | 欧洲和香港流行的高杠杆期权类产品，含失效价格。Highly leveraged derivative similar to a knock-out warrant; popular in HK and Europe. |
| **CFD (Contract for Difference)** | 差价合约 | 买卖双方约定就开仓价与平仓价之差进行现金结算，无需持有实际资产。Agreement to exchange the difference in price between opening and closing a position; no ownership of underlying. |
| **Stock Futures** | 股票期货 | 在未来特定日期以约定价格买卖指定股票的标准化期货合约。Standardized futures contract to buy or sell a specific stock at a future date and agreed price. |

## 4.4 指数产品 / Index Products

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **ETF (Exchange-Traded Fund)** | 交易所交易基金 | 在交易所上市交易的基金，通常追踪指数（被动型），费率低廉，流动性高。Fund listed on an exchange, typically tracking an index (passive); low fees, high liquidity. Examples: SPY (S&P 500), QQQ (Nasdaq), 2800.HK (Hang Seng). |
| **Index Futures** | 股指期货 | 对应某一股票指数的标准化期货合约，用于对冲或投机。Standardized futures contract on a stock index; used for hedging or speculation. Examples: S&P 500 E-mini, Hang Seng Futures, CSI 300 Futures (CFFEX). |
| **Index Options** | 指数期权 | 以股票指数为标的的期权合约，通常以现金结算。Options on a stock index; typically cash-settled. |
| **Total Return Swap (TRS) on Index** | 指数总收益掉期 | 一方支付固定/浮动利率，另一方支付指数总回报（含股息），无需持有实际指数成分股。One party pays a fixed/floating rate; the other pays the total return of an index (including dividends); synthetic index exposure. |
| **Smart Beta / Factor ETF** | 智能贝塔/因子ETF | 按特定因子（价值、动量、质量、低波动等）而非传统市值加权构建的ETF。ETF constructed by factor tilts (value, momentum, quality, low-volatility) rather than pure market-cap weighting. |
