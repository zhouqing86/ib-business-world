# Phase 10 — Hong Kong & China-Specific Terms

> **学习目标 / Learning Objectives**  
> 掌握香港和中国内地金融市场的特有机构、监管框架、金融工具及结构融资术语，包括SPV、沪深港通、债券通等。  
> Master HK and mainland China-specific institutions, regulation, instruments, and structured finance terms including SPVs, Stock Connect, and Bond Connect.

## 10.1 香港监管机构 / Hong Kong Regulatory Bodies

| 缩写 | 全称 | 中文 | 职能 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **HKMA** | Hong Kong Monetary Authority | 香港金融管理局 | 香港的央行职能机构（非正式）：管理外汇基金（港元联系汇率制度）、银行监管、制定货币政策。HK's de facto central bank: manages Exchange Fund (Linked Exchange Rate System peg), regulates banks, maintains monetary stability. |
| **SFC** | Securities and Futures Commission | 证券及期货事务监察委员会 | 监管香港证券和期货市场、持牌机构及集体投资计划（基金）。Regulates HK securities and futures markets, licensed intermediaries, and collective investment schemes. |
| **HKEX** | Hong Kong Exchanges and Clearing | 香港交易所 | 香港股票、债券和衍生品的上市及交易基础设施运营商；旗下包含联交所（SEHK）、期交所（HKFE）和香港中央结算（HKSCC）。Operates HK's stock, bond, and derivatives markets; subsidiaries include SEHK, HKFE, HKSCC. |
| **HKSCC** | Hong Kong Securities Clearing Company | 香港中央结算有限公司 | HKEX旗下子公司，运营香港中央结算系统（CCASS），负责港股的结算与托管。HKEX subsidiary; runs CCASS (Central Clearing and Settlement System) for HK securities. |
| **IA** | Insurance Authority | 保险业监管局 | 监管香港保险公司和持牌保险中介人。Regulates insurance companies and licensed insurance intermediaries in HK. |
| **MPFA** | Mandatory Provident Fund Schemes Authority | 强积金管理局 | 监管香港强制性公积金（MPF）制度。Regulates Hong Kong's Mandatory Provident Fund (MPF) retirement scheme. |

## 10.2 香港特有金融工具 / HK-Specific Financial Instruments

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Exchange Fund Bills (EFBs)** | 外汇基金票据 | 由HKMA代表香港政府发行的短期（91天、182天、364天）港元债务工具；票面利率参考HIBOR；常用于银行间流动性管理。Short-term HKD debt securities (91、182、364 days) issued by HKMA; HIBOR-linked coupons; used for interbank liquidity management. |
| **Exchange Fund Notes (EFNs)** | 外汇基金债券 | HKMA发行的中长期（2至10年）港元债券；被市场视为港元无风险利率基准。Medium-to-long-term (2–10 year) HKD bonds issued by HKMA; benchmark HKD risk-free rate. |
| **CBBC (Callable Bull/Bear Contract)** | 牛熊证 | 追踪标的资产（港股、恒生指数）的高杠杆衍生品；含强制回收（Mandatory Call）机制，当标的价格触及牛熊证的强制回收价时立即终止。Leveraged derivative tracking an underlying (HK stocks, HSI); featuring a Mandatory Call Event (MCE) when the underlying hits the call price. |
| **Inline Warrant** | 界内证 | 若标的资产在行权日收盘价处于设定的上下限价格之间，则获得固定收益；否则仅收回$0.25。Pays a fixed return if the underlying closes within a pre-set range (upper and lower strike) at expiry; pays HK$0.25 otherwise. |
| **Derivative Warrants** | 衍生权证 | 由发行机构（银行）发行的期权型产品，在联交所上市交易，分认购（Bull）和认沽（Bear）两类。Bank-issued option-like instruments listed on HKEX; bull (call) and bear (put) varieties. |

## 10.3 香港SPV与结构融资 / Hong Kong SPV & Structured Finance

在国际债券发行和证券化交易中，SPV是核心法律结构。香港/开曼群岛SPV被广泛用于亚洲国际债券发行。  
SPVs are central to international bond issuance and securitisation. HK/Cayman SPVs are widely used in Asian international bond deals.

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **SPV (Special Purpose Vehicle)** | 特殊目的载体 | 为特定金融交易（如债券发行、证券化）而设立的独立法律实体，实现资产与发起人的破产隔离。Separate legal entity created for a specific financial transaction (bond issuance, securitisation) to achieve bankruptcy remoteness from the originator. |
| **SPE (Special Purpose Entity)** | 特殊目的实体 | 与SPV同义；在不同会计和监管框架下可能有细微区别。Synonym for SPV; may have slightly different implications in specific accounting/regulatory contexts. |
| **Cayman Islands SPV** | 开曼群岛SPV | 大多数亚洲国际债/中资美元债（CMBS）通过在开曼群岛注册的SPV发行；开曼群岛税收优惠、法律体系成熟、受国际投资者认可。Most Asian international bonds are issued via Cayman-incorporated SPVs; favourable tax treatment, mature legal system, widely accepted by international investors. |
| **Orphan Structure** | 孤儿结构 | SPV的股权由独立慈善信托（charitable trust）持有，而非发起人；确保SPV在法律上完全独立于发起人，强化破产隔离。SPV equity held by an independent charitable trust (not the originator); maximises legal independence and bankruptcy remoteness from the originator. |
| **Bankruptcy Remoteness** | 破产隔离 | SPV在法律上与发起人分离，发起人破产不影响SPV资产；国际评级机构要求发行结构具备此特性。Legal separation of the SPV from the originator; originator's insolvency cannot affect SPV assets; required by international rating agencies. |
| **True Sale** | 真实出售 | 发起人将资产出售给SPV时须满足的法律标准：资产真实转移所有权，非担保融资安排；真实出售是实现破产隔离的前提。Legal standard requiring actual transfer of ownership of assets to the SPV (not merely a secured loan); prerequisite for bankruptcy remoteness. |
| **MTN Program** | 中期票据计划 | 允许发行人在预设框架下灵活多次发行不同期限和金额债券的发行机制，大幅降低每次发行的法律成本。Framework allowing an issuer to issue notes of varying tenors and sizes under pre-established documentation; reduces legal costs per issuance. |
| **EMTN (Euro Medium Term Note)** | 欧洲中期票据 | 在欧洲市场发行的中期票据，通常面向专业投资者；大多数亚洲国际债通过EMTN计划发行。Notes issued under an EMTN programme in European markets; most Asian international bonds are issued this way. |
| **Reg S** | 美国证券法规S | 允许在美国境外向非美国投资者出售证券，无需在SEC注册；亚洲国际债的标准发行方式。Exemption allowing securities to be sold outside the US to non-US persons without SEC registration; standard for Asian international bonds. |
| **Rule 144A** | 美国私募发行规则 | 允许向美国合格机构买家（QIBs）私募发行证券而无需公开注册；大型发行通常结合Reg S+144A双轨结构。Allows private placement of securities to US Qualified Institutional Buyers (QIBs) without public registration; large deals often use Reg S + 144A dual-tranche structure. |
| **Keepwell Deed** | 维好契约 | 中资发行人境内母公司向境外SPV提供的承诺：维持SPV的流动性和净资产；不构成法律上的担保但被市场接受为信用增强手段（法律效力争议较大）。Commitment by an onshore Chinese parent to maintain the offshore SPV's liquidity and net assets; not a legal guarantee but accepted as credit support (enforceability debated). |
| **EIPU (Equity Interest Purchase Undertaking)** | 股权收购承诺 | 中资境外债中，如SPV无法偿债，境内母公司承诺回购SPV股权；与Keepwell配套使用的信用增强结构。Undertaking by onshore parent to purchase the equity of the offshore SPV if it cannot service its debt; used alongside Keepwell as credit enhancement. |
| **Cross-Border Security** | 跨境担保 | 境内公司为境外债券提供担保或抵押；受SAFE（国家外汇管理局）审批和额度限制。Onshore Chinese company providing security for offshore debt; subject to SAFE approval and quota limitations. |

## 10.4 沪深港通与债券通 / Stock Connect & Bond Connect

| 英文 | 中文 | 说明 / Description |
|------|------|--------------------|
| **Stock Connect (沪深港通)** | 沪深港通 | 连接香港与内地股票市场的互联互通机制，允许两地投资者在对方市场买卖股票，无需QFII/RQFII额度。Mutual market access program linking HK and mainland China stock markets; allows investors on both sides to trade each other's eligible stocks without QFII/RQFII quotas. |
| **Northbound (北向通)** | 北向通 | 香港及境外投资者通过沪深港通买卖上海/深圳A股。HK and overseas investors buying SSE/SZSE-listed A-shares via Stock Connect. |
| **Southbound (南向通)** | 南向通 | 内地中国投资者通过沪深港通买卖香港上市股票。Mainland China investors buying HKEX-listed stocks via Stock Connect. |
| **Daily Quota Limit** | 每日额度 | 沪深港通每日净买入额度（北向每日额度520亿人民币；南向每日额度420亿港元）。Daily net buy quota; Northbound: RMB 52bn; Southbound: HKD 42bn. |
| **Bond Connect (债券通)** | 债券通 | 允许境外机构投资者通过香港直接进入中国银行间债券市场（CIBM）买卖债券，无需在境内开户。Allows overseas investors to access China's interbank bond market (CIBM) via Hong Kong without setting up onshore accounts. |
| **Northbound Bond Connect** | 北向债券通 | 境外投资者买卖在岸人民币债券（主要是政府债和政策行债）。Overseas investors buying onshore RMB bonds (mainly CGBs and policy bank bonds). |
| **Swap Connect (互换通)** | 互换通 | 允许境外机构投资者通过香港进入内地银行间利率互换（IRS）市场，对冲境内债券持仓的利率风险。Enables overseas investors to access China's onshore interest rate swap (IRS) market via HK; allows hedging of onshore bond interest rate risk. |
| **QFII** | 合格境外机构投资者 | 允许获批的境外机构直接在内地资本市场投资；早于沪深港通建立，需申请资格和额度。Allows approved foreign institutions to invest directly in China's domestic capital markets; predates Stock Connect; requires qualification and quota. |
| **RQFII** | 人民币合格境外机构投资者 | QFII的人民币版本，以离岸人民币（CNH）进行投资。RMB version of QFII; invests using offshore RMB (CNH). |
| **QDII** | 合格境内机构投资者 | 允许境内机构将资金投资于境外资产市场（股票、债券、基金）的机制。Allows domestic Chinese institutions to invest in overseas financial assets within approved quotas. |
| **QFLP** | 合格境外有限合伙人 | 允许境外机构以有限合伙人（LP）形式直接投资中国境内私募股权/创业投资基金。Allows foreign institutions to invest as limited partners in onshore private equity/VC funds in China. |

## 10.5 中国境内市场与机构 / China Onshore Market & Institutions

| 缩写 | 全称 | 中文 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|------|
| **PBOC** | People's Bank of China | 中国人民银行 | 中国中央银行：制定货币政策、发行人民币、监管银行间市场、设定LPR和存款准备金率。China's central bank; sets monetary policy, issues RMB, oversees interbank markets, sets LPR and RRR. |
| **CBIRC / NFRA** | China Banking and Insurance Regulatory Commission / National Financial Regulatory Administration | 国家金融监督管理总局 | 监管中国银行业和保险业（2023年起整合为NFRA）。Supervises Chinese banking and insurance industries; merged into NFRA in 2023. |
| **CSRC** | China Securities Regulatory Commission | 中国证券监督管理委员会 | 监管中国资本市场（股票、债券、期货）及相关机构。Regulates China's capital markets (equities, bonds, futures) and market participants. |
| **SAFE** | State Administration of Foreign Exchange | 国家外汇管理局 | 管理中国外汇储备、监管跨境资本流动、发放QFII/QDII额度。Manages China's FX reserves; regulates cross-border capital flows; issues QFII/QDII quotas. |
| **NAFMII** | National Association of Financial Market Institutional Investors | 中国银行间市场交易商协会 | 银行间市场自律组织，负责银行间债券（如中期票据MTN、短期融资券CP）的注册和行为规范。SRO for the interbank market; registers interbank bonds (MTNs, CP); conducts market regulation. |
| **CFETS** | China Foreign Exchange Trade System | 中国外汇交易中心 | 运营境内外汇、债券和货币市场的电子交易基础设施；是银行间市场的核心交易平台。Electronic trading infrastructure for onshore FX, bond, and money markets; backbone of the Chinese interbank market. |
| **CIBM** | China Interbank Bond Market | 中国银行间债券市场 | 中国最大的债券市场（占债券市场总量约85%），主要参与者为银行和机构投资者，通过CFETS进行场外交易。China's largest bond market (~85% of total); OTC market for banks and institutions; accessed via CFETS. |
| **NCD** | Negotiable Certificate of Deposit | 同业存单 | 银行发行的可交易定期存款凭证，是中国货币市场的重要工具；收益率通常参考SHIBOR。Tradeable time deposit receipt issued by banks; important Chinese money market instrument; yield typically referenced to SHIBOR. |
| **MLF** | Medium-term Lending Facility | 中期借贷便利 | PBOC向金融机构提供中期资金（通常1年期）的政策工具；MLF利率是LPR的基准。PBOC tool providing medium-term liquidity (typically 1-year) to financial institutions; MLF rate is the basis for LPR. |
| **LPR** | Loan Prime Rate | 贷款市场报价利率 | 中国基准贷款利率，由18家报价行在MLF利率基础上加点报价、由PBOC每月公布；分1年期和5年以上两档（5年以上主要影响房贷）。China's benchmark lending rate; quoted by 18 banks based on MLF rate; published monthly by PBOC; 1-year and 5-year+ tenors. |
| **DR007** | — | 7天期存款类机构质押式回购加权利率 | 中国货币市场的核心短期利率，反映银行间资金松紧程度；是利率曲线的短端锚点。Key short-term rate in China's money market; reflects interbank liquidity conditions; the short-end anchor of the Chinese yield curve. |
| **CGBs** | China Government Bonds | 中国国债 | 由财政部发行的人民币主权债券，通过CFETS在银行间市场交易；纳入主要国际债券指数（彭博巴克莱、富时罗素）。RMB sovereign bonds issued by China's Ministry of Finance; traded on CIBM via CFETS; included in major international bond indices. |
